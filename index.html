Cupcake Party

You have been tasked with bringing cupcakes to the party. You will be given a random number at the start of the game to let you know how many guests their will be. This is the number of cupcakes you need to bring to the party.

There are four cupcakes below. By clicking on a cupcake you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. Some cupcakes are worth more than others.

You win the game by matching your total score to a the number of cupcakes needed. You lose the game if your total score goes above the cupcakes needed as you brought too many to the party.

The value of each cupcake is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of cupcakes needed.
